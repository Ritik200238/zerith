## Rules of engagement

1. **Hand-craft every email.** Variant A is a skeleton, not a template — replace `{{foundation}}` AND add 1–2 sentences tying it to something they did this month.
2. **5 sends per week, max.** This is a quality game. Scale only after 3 conversions to call.
3. **One follow-up per target.** Two if they engaged then went silent.
4. **Log every reply verbatim** in a Status notes block under the row. Objections are gold.

## Conversion bar (so you know when to invest more)

- Send 15 → expect 2–4 replies → 1–2 calls → 0–1 paid pilot.
- If after 15 sends you have <2 replies: the email or the targeting is wrong. Stop, fix, restart.
- If after 30 calls you have 0 pilots: the *product positioning* is wrong, not the outreach.
